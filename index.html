About me

I am currently an associate professor at the Institute of Mathematics, Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Previously, I held postdoctoral positions at MIT, where I was mentored by Zhiwei Yun, and at the University of Michigan, where I was mentored by Tasho Kaletha.

Research interests

Number theory and representation theory, with a particular interest in theta correspondence and the (relative) Langlands program.
