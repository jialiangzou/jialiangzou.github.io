About

About me

I am currently a research associate at the Institute of Mathematics, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at MIT, mentored by Zhiwei Yun, and at the University of Michigan, mentored by Tasho Kaletha. Before coming to Ann Arbor, I was a Ph.D. student at the National University of Singapore under the supervision of Wee Teck Gan and Chengbo Zhu.
